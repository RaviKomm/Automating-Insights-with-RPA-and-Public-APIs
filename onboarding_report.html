Data Pipeline

Source 1 — Open-Meteo Forecast API: 7-day daily weather for 5 cities (temperature max/min in °C converted to °F, precipitation in mm, wind speed in km/h). Temperatures converted using standard formula (°C × 9/5 + 32).

Source 2 — Open-Meteo Air Quality API: Hourly UV index and PM10 particulate readings aggregated to daily maximums for worst-case risk assessment.

Source 3 — JSONPlaceholder Users API: 10 simulated new hire records assigned to cities in round-robin order (2 per city). Each hire's city risk score drives the IN-PERSON vs REMOTE recommendation using a 40% high-risk-day threshold per HR policy.

Transformations: Each day flagged HIGH RISK if any threshold exceeded (temp < 20°F, temp > 95°F, precip > 20mm, wind > 50 km/h, UV > 8, PM10 > 50). City risk score = % of 7 forecast days classified HIGH RISK.

ML Enhancement Opportunities
